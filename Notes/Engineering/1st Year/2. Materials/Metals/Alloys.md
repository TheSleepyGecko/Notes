
- Alloys are mixtures of two or more metals.

| Alloy  | Mixture       | Application                   |
| ------ | ------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Brass  | Copper + Zinc | Hinges and musical equipments |
| Bronze | Copper + Tin  | Medals and statues            |
| Solder | Lead + Tin    | Electrical connections        |
