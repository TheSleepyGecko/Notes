Energy - `The ability to do work. The unit of energy is the Joule`
Heat/Thermal Energy - `Anything with a temperature above absolute zero (-273C) has heat energy.`
Kinetic Energy - `Anythign that moves has kinetic enegry.`
Sound Energy - `Anything noisy gives off sound energy.`
Light/Solar Energy - `Anything luminous gives off light energy.`
Chemical Energy - `Anything with stored energy which can be released by a chemical reaction has chemical energy.`
Electrical Energy - `Wherever there is a current flowing, there is electrical energy.`
Potential Energy - `Anything that can change position or shape will have potential energy.`
Nuclear Energy - `Energy released from nuclear reactions.`
Magnetic Energy - `Electrical motors causing movement are examples of magnetic energy.`
